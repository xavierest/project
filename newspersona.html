Persona 3 Portable & Persona 4 Gold are Now Available on Nintendo Switch

Revisit the story again on the Nintendo Switch

Posted on: February 26, 2023 by Xavi

Now you can play Persona 3 Portable & Persona 4 Golden on the Nintendo Switch. You can purchase them either in a bundle or separately. As a bundle, they cost $39.49, and separately they cost $19.99 each.

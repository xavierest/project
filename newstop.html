Anticipated Games for 2023: Part I

List of upcoming games that we can’t wait to play…

Octropath Traveler II

Posted on: February 26, 2023 by Xavi

Take an adventure with eight travelers who venture into the world of Solistia for their own reasons. There’s a bustling land to explore in this beautifully pixelated world.

Resident Evil 4 Remake

Resident Evil 4 Remake is a reimagined version of the original storyline. This story takes place 6 years after the biological disaster that happened in Raccoon City. Leon is sent to rescue the president’s daughter from a secluded European village.

The Legend of Zelda: tears of Kingdom

In this sequel, following the events of The Legend of Zelda: breath of the wild, Link sets into a new adventure that includes the skies above Hyrule.

Final Fantasy XVI

Final Fantasy XVI is the sixteenth main installment from the Final Fantasy series, exclusive for the PlayStation 5. The story takes place on the continent of Valisthea, where Clive Rosfield is put on a path of revenge after the tragic attack on the Dutchy.

The journey back to Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn

A concise perspective of a journey back to the beginning...

Posted on: February 26, 2023 by Xavi

Final Fantasy XIV: A Realm Reborn's first storyline can be polarizing. I decided to revisit the story after years of experiencing the changes the team has made. I can say, for sure, the quality of life improvements has made the experience more manageable.

The addition of trusts for main story dungeons is a welcome addition. As a DPS, you won’t have to wait for a party to accomplish these dungeons. Also, the last-story dungeons for A Realm Reborn have gotten revamped. They won’t last an hour like they used to. Now they are broken into smaller chunks, and you can even take a nice break between dungeons.

However, even with the quests the team eliminated, it’s still the longest storyline up to date in the game Additionally, the fact that many cutscenes aren’t voice-acted makes the experience feel like you are not progressing

Should you start this game after all the content they have added? I would say yes. The story is more enjoyable now than ever. After you finish A Realm Reborn, you will start one of the best expansions in Final Fantasy XIV, Heavensward.
